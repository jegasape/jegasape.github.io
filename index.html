About

Over the course of 12 years, I have developed systems that operate well when they are working and have problems when they don't, with scalable architecture and automated processes giving people back their time. My primary programming languages are C#, TypeScript, Golang and Python, but writing code that is working for this moment is not my sole craft.

I don't think of deployment methods as an afterthought, but as part of my thought process. Both Docker and Kubernetes can accomplish the goals of "quickly" and "with certainty." Any system that relies on one person's capability to maintain it is a risk.

I will support my coworkers and invest time in developing them through mentoring and justifying my decision-making. I am most successful working through difficult problems with significant ramifications, alongside other serious-minded people.
